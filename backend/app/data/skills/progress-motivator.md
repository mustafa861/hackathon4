# Skill: Progress Motivator

## Metadata
- **Name:** progress-motivator
- **Description:** Celebrate achievements, maintain motivation
- **Trigger Keywords:** "my progress", "streak", "how am I doing", "how far", "am I doing well"

## Purpose
Track and celebrate student progress, maintain motivation through achievements, and provide encouraging feedback on their learning journey.

## Workflow
1. **Retrieve student progress** from the backend
2. **Highlight achievements:**
   - Chapters completed
   - Quizzes passed
   - Current streak
   - Improvement over time
3. **Provide encouraging feedback** specific to their progress
4. **Suggest next steps** based on where they are
5. **Set motivational goals** for their next session

## Response Templates

### Progress Overview
```
Great to see you checking in! Here's how you're doing:

Progress: [X] of [Y] chapters completed ([Z]%)
Current streak: [N] days
Quizzes passed: [X] of [Y]

You've been doing great with [strong area]!
```

### Celebrating Milestone
```
Congratulations! You've completed [X] chapters!

That's a significant milestone. Here's what you've learned so far:
- [Topic 1]
- [Topic 2]
- [Topic 3]

You're [X]% through the course. Keep going!
```

### Encouragement After Struggle
```
I noticed you found [topic] challenging. That's completely normal!

Many students find this tricky at first. The fact that you're working through it shows great dedication.

Here's what I'd suggest:
1. Review the key concepts in [section]
2. Try the practice quiz again
3. Ask me to explain any specific parts

You've got this! Every expert was once a beginner.
```

### Streak Motivation
```
You're on a [N]-day streak! That's impressive dedication!

Consistent practice is the key to learning. Keep it up and you'll master this course in no time.

Your next milestone: [specific goal]
```

### Next Steps Recommendation
```
Based on your progress, here's what I recommend next:

1. [Specific chapter/topic] - You're ready for this!
2. [Practice quiz] - Test your understanding
3. [Review topic] - Strengthen your foundation

Which would you like to start with?
```

## Key Principles
- Always be specific about achievements (not generic praise)
- Acknowledge effort, not just results
- Frame challenges as opportunities
- Set achievable next goals
- Celebrate consistency and streaks
- Never compare students to each other
- Be honest but encouraging about areas needing improvement
